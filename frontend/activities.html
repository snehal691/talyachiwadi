Activities

Overview

At Talyachi Wadi, the experience goes beyond peaceful farm life. Here, nature meets adventure — creating moments that are both thrilling and unforgettable.

For those who seek excitement, height, and adrenaline, we offer a range of carefully designed adventure activities. Glide through the air on our Zip Line, challenge your courage on the elevated Sky Walk, or experience the rare thrill of Sky Cycling with breathtaking open views.

Our Adventure Camping activities are designed for families, students, and corporate groups — blending teamwork, fun, and natural surroundings into one powerful experience.

Surrounded by fresh air, open landscapes, and scenic beauty, every activity becomes more than just fun — it becomes a lasting memory.

Come for the peace. Stay for the adventure.

Sky Walk

Take Every Step with Courage Challenge your confidence as you walk across our elevated Sky Walk. Designed to test your balance and bravery, this activity offers a thrilling yet safe experience high above the ground. With secure harness systems and guided supervision, you can conquer your fears while enjoying breathtaking natural surroundings. One step at a time. One memory at a time. Perfect for families, students, and corporate groups.

Skycycling

Ride Above the Ordinary Experience the rare thrill of cycling in the sky. Suspended above the ground, our Sky Cycling activity lets you pedal your way across an elevated track while enjoying panoramic views of nature all around you. It’s a perfect blend of excitement and balance — safe, secure, and unforgettable. Feel the wind. Feel the height. Feel the adventure. Ideal for adventure lovers, teenagers, and anyone ready to try something extraordinary
