Our Story

From 2 Trees to 10,000+: A Vision of Pure Oxygen. In 2000, visionary Gopal Patil embarked on a mission to create a legacy. What started with just two trees is now a thriving 35-acre forest featuring over 10,000 trees, 3 man-made lakes, and a commitment to poison-free natural farming.

Talyachi Wadi Resort is a unique eco-resort located on the Nashik–Trimbakeshwar Road. Spread across 35 acres of lush greenery, the resort is a living example of how barren land can be transformed into a thriving natural ecosystem. What began with just two trees has now grown into a forest of over 10,000 trees, three man-made lakes, and natural farming practices. Our resort offers a peaceful escape from city life while keeping you connected to nature.
